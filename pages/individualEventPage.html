In Conversation with Lauren Epsom

10/12/2020
17:00
5.00

Register Here

You heard that right! We invite the public to join us for this conversation with post-internet artist and poet Lauren Epsom, on December 10th at 5pm. We will be talking about a range of issues - what it means to be a writer on the web, what the future of literature looks like and how she invented her own digital language.
